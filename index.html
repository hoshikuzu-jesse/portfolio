Terms Of Service

  1. Please be respectful. If you are being rude I will turn down your commission.
  2. Payment will be made via PayPal for USD.
  3. The PSD file or completed illustration will not be delivered until full payment is confirmed.
  4. The deadline will be discussed before the commission is started, if it needs to be shortened mid-commission an extra fee will be charged.
  5. Changes won't be charged extra while in the sketching phase.
  6. If the client needs any big change regarding character design after the actual model or illustration has been started, an extra fee will be applied. The same goes if the commission is already finished.

    7. VTuber Only

  7. The model cannot be sold for a price higher than the original.
  8. The artist should be credited at least in one place when the model is being used in a public platform.
  9. Both the artist and the rigger have the right to post their work as an achievement in social media and their portfolio after the commissioner has publicly shown the model.
  10. I don't take responsibility for any troublesome use of the model by the commissioner.
